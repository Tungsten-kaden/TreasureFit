About Us



Instagram:@treasure_lesinya


TreasureFit was founded in early 2024 with a vision to empower individuals to achieve their fitness goals through accessible and inclusive programs. Recognizing the importance of community and support, TreasureFit was born out of a desire to create a space where people could feel comfortable, motivated, and empowered on their fitness journeys.

From humble beginnings, TreasureFit has grown into a thriving fitness organization, offering a range of programs to cater to diverse needs and fitness levels. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, TreasureFit provides the tools, resources, and support you need to succeed.

  • Mission Statement: To empower individuals to achieve their fitness goals through accessible, inclusive, and inspiring programs, fostering a community of support and celebrating every milestone.

  • Vision Statement: To be the leading fitness organization in the community, known for its innovative approach, dedication to client success, and positive impact on overall well-being.

    • Early 2025: Founded by Lesinya Treasure with a vision to create an inclusive and supportive fitness community.
